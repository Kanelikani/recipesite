Parmesan Garlic Ramen

This is a very simple recipe that spices up a regular packet of ramen.

Ingredients
  • Ramen noodles
  • Unsalted butter
  • Minced garlic
  • Shredded parmesan cheese
  • Dried Italian seasoning

Steps
  • Cook your ramen in a regular fashion.
  • Melt butter in a skillet. Add garlic and let cook untill fragrant.
  • Stir parmesan cheese, Italian seasoning and reserved cooking water. Let cook untill cheese is melted.
  • Add noodles and done!
