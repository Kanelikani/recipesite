Simple BBQ Ribs

This is a recipe to some very simple and easy to make BBQ ribs.

Ingredients
  • Ribs
  • Kosher salt
  • Garlic powder
  • Ground black pepper
  • Barbeque sauce
Steps
  • Boil the ribs in water that has salt, pepper and garlic powder in it for about 40 minutes.
  • Take the ribs out of the pot once done boiling and lay them on a tray.
  • Pour barbeque sauce over ribs and cover the dish with aluminium foil.
  • Bake in the oven for about 1Â½ hours.
